<Question> 
 In this study, did authors aim at studying Adults with ASD ?

<All Answers to this Question>

Final answers statistics: A total of 13 papers answered yes to this question,  whereas 83 papers answered no. Here is the information of the papers that answered yes:

Answer:Yes. The study involved an 8-year-old boy in third grade with Level 3 autism.
